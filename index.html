Hyperspace Graph Paper

Hyperspace Graph Paper (HSGP) visualises high-dimensional Boolean spaces. It has the unusual property of allowing relatively quick navigation in multiple dimensions, and is also excellent for viewing certain types of structures.

HSGP has been used in several publications to understand the structure of fitness landscapes for genetic algorithms, and to understand attractor basin structure in Boolean regulatory networks.
